The Final Hour at the Count’s Manor

This one-shot is a halloween themed one-shot that has the freedom to be made into any theme for your party. You can take a comedic spin on this of can go full horror the choice is completely yours to make. With mind-bending puzzles, intense battles, and confusing mazes all ending with a ferocious battle against The Count; a powerful vampire with hidden secrets laying around his lair, this One-Shot is sure to immerse your players in a halloween themed atmosphere.

This game will consist of two puzzles, 1 fight scene and a maze before the epic throwdown at the end.

Game Setup

The players could start of in the back of a cart being taken to the manor and forced to go in there (What I did), however there are other ways you could start this adventure such as

  • Brave adventurers who want to explore the unknown
  • Kidnapped prisoners forced to enter the mansion
