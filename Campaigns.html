Campaigns

Curse of the Cards

Following an Alice in Borderland style story, this captivating card-collecting story promises to keep your players enthralled as they immerse themselves in a realm where each card is vital for survival. With high-stakes challenges, unexpected twists, and mysterious games that blurs the boundaries between reality and illusion, players will not cease their interest.

Featuring 52 distinct challenges, each associated with a card from the deck, every trial assesses the characters’ intelligence, strength, and ethics in difficult situations. Throughout the adventure, a varied ensemble of well-built characters will build alliances with the party. The players will confront betrayals, and reveal secrets that endanger not only their lives but also the very essence of the games themselves.
