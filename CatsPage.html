Cat Care Tips

  • Fresh water and balanced food are essential — change water daily and follow feeding recommendations for your cat's age and weight.
  • Regular vet checkups, vaccines, and parasite control keep cats healthy.
  • Provide toys, scratching posts, and safe places to climb to keep your cat mentally and physically active.
  • Spay or neuter your pet to reduce unwanted behaviors and help control the cat population.

Fun Cat Facts

  • Cats can make over 100 different vocal sounds; dogs can make about 10.
  • A group of cats is called a clowder.
  • Most cats sleep 12-16 hours a day — they're professional nappers.
