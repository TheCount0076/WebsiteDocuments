About me

I am The Count, through this website you can find out much about me and what I do here on Planet Earth.

Starting off I like to make Dungeon and Dragons Campaigns for my own group! You can find many of them here on my site.
I also make Card Games that are mostly adaptations of pre-existing card games but there are a few original ones out there!
All of my campaigns and card games are free of use and free to modify yourself! On my campaign page you may leave reviews and comments on how to improve these campaigns for other users!
